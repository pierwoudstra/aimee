Scarecrows, 2021
Presentation at Gerrit Rietveld Academy, The Netherlands




A fascination for scarecrows and their ability to frighten and protect within an urban environment. Within this research scarecrows became a gentle form of self protection and evaluation. They are subjects to their surrounding and maker, lethargic compagnions.
