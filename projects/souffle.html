Souffle ///// Milkbottle, 2021
Presentation at Sound Light Colour Studio, The Netherlands




Based on two texts and an archive called ‘a cat on your lap, uninvited’ exploring the appropria- tion of binary language this short performance is touching upon subjects as protecting, covering, uncovering, oppression and historical stigma’s about femininity and masculinity.
