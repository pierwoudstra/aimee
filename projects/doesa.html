‘Does a Harlequins Vagus nerve resemble the fossil of a bat?’, 2025
Presentation at Centrale de Fies, Italy




‘Does a Harlequins Vagus nerve resemble the fossil of a bat?’ Attempts to develop lexicons of form, material, and texture that entwine the poetic and political discourse on the queer grotesque. By addressing the mouth-anus axis through the vagus nerve, echolocation as a subversive way of communicating, tongue twisters and puppeteering the project examens and attempts to reinvent societies view on abjection and bodies in transformation.

Performance by Dory Ikonen
